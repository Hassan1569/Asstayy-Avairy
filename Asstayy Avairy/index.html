Our Story

Born From a Lifelong Love
of Birds

Asstayyy Aviary began in 2012 as a small backyard passion project in Karachi — a few pairs of doves, a hand-built cage, and a deep fascination with avian behaviour. Over the years, that passion grew into one of Karachi's most trusted bird breeding operations.

We believe every bird deserves to be raised in a clean, enriching environment and every buyer deserves honest, expert guidance. That philosophy hasn't changed since day one.

Get in Touch

Our Journey

How We Grew

2012
The First Pair

We started with two pairs of diamond doves in a small backyard aviary in Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi. Word spread quickly among neighbours.

2015
Lovebirds Join the Family

Demand for colourful companion birds led us to introduce peach-faced and blue mutation lovebirds. Our first 50 pairs sold within weeks.

2018
Cockatiels & Expansion

We expanded to a dedicated aviary space and introduced cockatiels — our most popular breed to date. Full-time veterinary partnership established.

2024
Going Digital

Launched online enquiries and delivery services citywide. Now serving over 200 happy bird families across Karachi.
